Who We Are

Founded by Radi B. Fontanoza and Jhim Jastine Lazatin in Marikina City, Nigabytes is dedicated to offering high-quality, affordable computer accessories, including keyboards, mice, and printers. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction drives us to continually explore the impact of our products on the local market and seek out new opportunities for innovation.

Our mission is to empower gamers, professionals, and computer enthusiasts to take their skills to the next level. We believe that everyone deserves access to the best computer peripherals, regardless of their budget or technical expertise. That's why we're committed to offering a wide range of products from top brands and our own in-house label, all at competitive prices.

At Nigabytes, our vision is to be the go-to destination for computer enthusiasts and professionals who demand the best peripherals to enhance their computing experience. We envision a future where everyone has access to high-quality, affordable, and innovative computer hardware that helps them achieve their goals and pursue their passions.
